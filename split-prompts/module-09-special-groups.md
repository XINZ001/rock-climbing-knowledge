### 9-1. kp-growth-plate-concerns
- **原始来源**: kp-growth-plate-concerns
- **标题**: 生长板损伤风险 / Growth Plate Concerns
- **布局类型**: Type F — 解剖/医学（健康 vs 受伤并排）
- **Prompt**: Instructional climbing diagram, clean line art style, minimal colors (forest green #4A7C59 and warm gray), white background. LEFT: anatomical side view of an adolescent finger showing three phalanx bones (distal, middle, proximal) with growth plates highlighted in forest green at PIP and MCP joints — lighter shading bands within the bone representing cartilaginous growth zones, NOT pulleys, NOT ligaments. Child proportions: proportionally larger hand relative to body (1/5 head-to-body ratio if full figure shown). RIGHT: same finger in full crimp on a small hold, stress arrows concentrated at the growth plate zones. Warning triangle symbol. Labels: "Growth Plate (epiphyseal plate)," "PIP joint," "MCP joint," "stress zone." Annotation: "do NOT confuse growth plates with pulleys — growth plates are INSIDE the bone ends, not around the tendon." Side view. Consistent style: simple human figures with no facial features, clean thin annotation arrows, sans-serif labels in English, generous white space margins on all sides. No background scenery or decorative elements.
