**Three core strategies:**
1. Playing to strengths — understand personal strengths; identify long-term changeable weaknesses
2. Building performance pyramids — solid foundations through progressive grades + diverse experience
3. Strategic performance methods — techniques suited to specific targets

**Career overview:**
3 routes at 9b+, 7 routes at 9b, multiple IFSC world cup victories. Started competing at age 14.

**Training specifics from multiple sources:**
- Trains on hangboard during rest days
- Rests 40–60 min between climbing attempts
- Attempts routes 2–3 times per day
- Balances bouldering and sport climbing sessions
- Topics from Lattice podcast: balance between bouldering and sport climbing; how much fingerboard vs wall climbing; managing fear of heights; seasonal goal identification

**Route accomplishments:**
First ascents / repeats: Lapsus (9b), Change, Bibliographie, Perfecto Mundo (all 9b+)
Note: Ghisolfi is also the setter of Erebor — the route Laura Rogora first-ascented at 9b/+.
