### 原文全文

**On professional path:**
Started climbing 2006 as a child, no initial professional aspirations. Junior World Champion lead 2012.

**On university + climbing balance:**
Completed degree at Faculty of Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana. "Most things can be done remotely, which helped me the most." Core mindset: "If you want to be a professional athlete you have to understand that there are things you will have to say no to."

**On Paris 2024 format (speed removal):**
"Definitely a small win." Bouldering is his strength; "Weakness is for sure my endurance and this will be the main thing to improve till the qualifiers."

**Training philosophy shift:**
Previously trained mostly alone + team sessions. Changed approach: train collaboratively with other athletes as much as possible.

**Slovenian team culture:**
"Being a member of the national team feels like a family because we have been through so much together."

**Future goals:**
Olympic qualification (primary). Then: win World Cup bouldering event; climb 9a outdoor; complete 8c boulder (Bügeleisen, Maltatal).

**Advice for beginners:**
"Learn to climb with your hips open." Warns against inward knee rotation as hard-to-fix habit.
