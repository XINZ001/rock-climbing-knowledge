### 原文全文（摘要版）

Colin Duffy (USA, b. 2003) ranked 7th in world men's boulder and lead in 2023. Applies mathematical problem-solving approach to climbing: "My goal is to figure out the solution." Studies Computer Science / Engineering background at University of Colorado Boulder while competing.

Key training notes from multiple sources:
- Trains with coach Chris Danielson
- Emphasizes building strong calluses and finger skin management
- 2024 Paris: finished 4th (missed podium by one hold, Toby Roberts beat Sorato Anraku for gold, Schubert bronze)
- Pre-Paris mindset: "climbing confidently and smartly rather than focusing on placement results"
