### 原文全文

Laura Rogora (Italy, b. 2001) became the first woman to redpoint a 9b/+ route: Erebor at Eremo di San Paolo, Italy (October 2021).

Previous women's record: 9b held by Angy Eiter (Austria) and Julia Chanourdie (France). Rogora had previously climbed 9b in July 2020: Ali Hulk Extension Total Sit Start.

Prior 9a+ ascents: Pure Dreaming Plus, The Bow, Terapia d'Urto. More than 10 routes at 9th French grade total.

**Rogora's quote:**
"It was an honor to do the first iteration of the most difficult route in Italy."

**Stefano Ghisolfi (route setter) to Rogora:**
"Bibliography isn't that much more difficult. If you haven't booked your vacation for 2022 yet, then take my advice." (Bibliography = 9c)

**Additional achievements (from search):**
- First woman to onsight 8c+ (Ultimate Sacrifice, Gorges du Loup — 2024)
- Also onsighted 8c alongside Janja Garnbret
- Two-time Olympic competitor (Tokyo, Paris)
