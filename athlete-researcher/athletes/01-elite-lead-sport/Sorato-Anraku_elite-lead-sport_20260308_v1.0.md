### 原文全文

**Profile:**
16-year-old high school student in Yachiyo, Chiba Prefecture. Height: 1.68m, wingspan: 1.82m. Quiet, unassuming daily persona — "as soon as he scales a wall, few in the world are better."

**Diet:** junk food, ramen, fried chicken, avoids vegetables (self-described typical teenager).

**Historic 2023 season:**
First climber to win Overall World Cup in both boulder AND lead in a single debut season.

**Coach's insight (Hiroshi Yasui):**
"He's got very good experience in Japan. We have a lot of competitions with a lot of good climbers, and a good balance of training and competition."

**Competition philosophy:**
"I'm not aiming for the overall World Cup Series, but I'm aiming to win every event. Then I guess the overall will take care of itself."

**Role model:**
Admires Mejdi Schalck (FRA) for performing consistently under pressure.

**Olympic motivation:**
"You become famous when lots of people see you on TV. I want to spread climbing from my hometown by winning World Cups but I think the Olympics is the fastest way to get your sport out there and recognised."

**Background:**
Father Takeshi introduced climbing when he was in 2nd grade at local gym. Name meaning: "Sorato" = outer space + Big Dipper constellation (father's interest).
