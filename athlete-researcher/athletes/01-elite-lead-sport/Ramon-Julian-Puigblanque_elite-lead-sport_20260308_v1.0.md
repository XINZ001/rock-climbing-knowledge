### 原文全文

**Background:**
Born January 19, 1981, Lleida, Catalonia, Spain. Started climbing at age 7. Two-time World Champion (Arco 2011, Aviles 2007), twice European champion, five Rock Master victories, World Cup Lead 2010 winner. First free ascent of La Rambla (9a+) at Siurana in 2003 — one of the first 9a+ in history.

**On 2013 season:**
"The season started really well, I was very motivated and the results were coming, at least for the other competitions." Inconsistency at World Cup stage after poor performances in South Korea and Valencia.

**On World Games victory (only missing title):**
"It was the only title I was missing and it was great to get it." Four-year wait created substantial pressure.

**On Rock Master record (7 victories):**
"It's a competition I feel I can always perform well at, the setting is genius, the audience is very pleasant."

**On longevity at 30+:**
"I keep on competing because I feel strong... Physically I feel like somebody in his 20s."

**On training approach:**
Prefers rope climbing. Trains at boulder wall due to facility limitations. Focuses on stretching for injury prevention and on-sight mental rehearsal.

**Future goal (2013):**
"I've been climbing 9a+ for a few years now and I feel like it's time to break the barrier." (Goal: 9b)
