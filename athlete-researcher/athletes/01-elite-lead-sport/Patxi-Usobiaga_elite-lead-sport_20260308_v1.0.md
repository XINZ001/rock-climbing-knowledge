### 原文全文

**Background:**
Born in Eibar, Basque Country, 1980. Inspired by climbing documentary as a child. Won World Cups 2006 & 2007, World Championship 2009 at age 28. Trained clients: Adam Ondra, Daniel Woods, Chris Sharma.

**Training philosophy:**
"It is only when your body is totally broken down that the training can really begin."
From his competition days: "My talent is being a masochist. My training totally breaks your body and your mind." (Film: Progression, 2009)

**On Sharma:**
Sharma described the experience: "You find this place where you're really pushing yourself…To get stronger involves suffering."

**Post-injury return:**
2011 severe car accident — herniated disc — forced retirement for nearly two years. Return in 2013 fundamentally changed his perspective on life beyond climbing.

**ATR method (Accumulation–Transformation–Realization):**
Regimen alternates strength-and-power routines with volume-and-endurance routines on back-to-back days. PUC Training cost in 2017: 550 Euros / 3 months.

**Core belief:**
"Trust and believe in oneself. Work well and hard, be methodical, disciplined. Fight to the end to try to achieve it, and most important, enjoy the process a lot."
