### 原文全文

**On his restrained celebration after winning gold:**
"I'm not the type who expresses my feelings strongly and I wasn't sure whether I had won. So I held back so as not to make a fool of myself."

**On catching up with his idols overnight:**
"It wasn't long ago that I watched videos by Adam Ondra or Jakob Schubert and dreamed of being like them. I was able to catch up almost overnight and I'm going to compete against them today. Fortunately, many of them are now very good friends."

**On the need for Spanish climbing infrastructure:**
"If my medal is not to remain an isolated case, we have to start working now and think not only of 2024, but also of 2028."

**Background:**
Born 2003. Started climbing with parents from very young age. At 13 (2016, upon Olympic announcement), moved to Barcelona to train under coach David Macia. Trains between High Performance Center and Chris Sharma's gym in Barcelona.

Olympic gold context: Won speed event but finished last in bouldering; fourth in lead gave him gold via combined scoring.
