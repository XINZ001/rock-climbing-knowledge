### 原文全文

**训练量：**
"I'm often alternating weeks with many sessions (4-5) and less intensive weeks with only 2 sessions, depending on how I feel. A session rarely takes longer than two hours."

波动式训练量：高强度周（4-5次）与低强度周（2次）交替，完全根据身体感觉调整。

**力量开发方法：**
"No, I don't do any other training or running, just a little bit of stretching... campusing boulders and campus boarding is everything I do to improve my body power!"

只靠抱石 + campus board 提升爆发力，零有氧训练，极少拉伸。

**优势与弱点：**
- 优势："I guess I'm good at athletic climbs, compression boulders and big moves"
- 弱点："I'm not flexible and another weakness is finger strength, I train it a lot but I'm probably too fat to get super strong on crimps :)"

**核心训练方法（Tyler Landman 传授）：**
"My tip for getting stronger is to climb steep, straight angles, 30-45 degrees; big moves between bad holds..." — 倾斜墙面 + 难把 + 大幅度动作的组合

**最佳攀岩温度偏好：**
接近 0° 时表现最佳；10-15° 时更舒适但非最强状态。

### 摘要 / 备注

> 极简力量训练哲学：只靠抱石+campus，无有氧，无健身。波动式训练量是保持长期高表现的方法。自认为是 compression/big move 型选手，对指力/柔韧性有清醒认识。
